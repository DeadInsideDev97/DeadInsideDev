FROM NOTHING

Climb the Ladder. Rule the Streets. Build Your Empire.

From Nothing is a gritty, high-stakes crime RPG set in a brutal, ultra-capitalist society where the odds are stacked against you — and the only way out is up. You start with nothing but ambition and raw street instinct. No legacy. No allies. No mercy.

Carve your path through a corrupt city by building your own gang from the ground up. Claim territory, run illegal operations, and fight rival crews for dominance one block at a time. But the criminal underworld isn’t your only battlefield — you’ll need to run fronts, launder dirty cash through legal businesses, and manage every move like a true boss to survive the system without getting swallowed by it.

As you rise, you'll face hard choices and constant danger. Will you be a ruthless kingpin feared by all, or a calculating mastermind hiding behind clean suits and storefronts?

Key Features:

  • 🔫 Territory Control: Take over blocks, defend your turf, and expand your criminal empire.
  • 💵 Crime Meets Capitalism: Launder money through legit businesses and exploit the economy to your advantage.
  • 🥪 Survival Mechanics: Stay fed, rested, and alive — hunger and fatigue can kill just as fast as a bullet.
  • 🧠 Strategic Progression: Manage your gang, assign roles, upgrade skills, and make alliances or enemies.
  • 🏙️ A Living, Breathing City: Dynamic events, patrolling cops, rival gangs, and shifting opportunities make every decision matter.
  • 🗣️ Choices With Weight: Dialogue, missions, and alliances shape your rise — and your legacy.

In this city, nobody hands you power. You take it.
Will you rise to the top — or die trying?
