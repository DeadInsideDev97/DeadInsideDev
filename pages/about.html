Background

My journey into game development began just after high school, sparked by a curiosity and a love for interactive worlds. What started as a casual hobby—tinkering with basic mechanics, experimenting with level design, and exploring digital art—quickly became a creative outlet I couldn’t stay away from. I found joy in building immersive experiences, the kind of games that draw you in and make you forget the world around you. As I entered the tech industry professionally, I was exposed to cutting-edge tools and systems, but I kept coming back to one undeniable truth: my real passion lies in creating. Not just code or systems, but experiences—worlds that feel alive, stories that resonate, and gameplay that keeps players engaged for hours on end. Now, I’m pursuing game development with purpose and dedication. My goal is to create games that don’t just entertain, but captivate—games where you can get lost, discover something new every time you play, and walk away feeling like you’ve lived a whole other life.
